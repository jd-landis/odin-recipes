The Perfect Strip Steak


A Few Simple Ingredients Can Go A Long Way.

Cooking a steak at home can seem daunting to some. The truth is, it's a simple process with a few simple ingredients.
This page will show you step by step how to sear and baste a New York strip steak and hopefully convience you that it's easy enough to give it a try yourself!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Pepper

The Process
As stated before, this is a simple process that demands quality ingredients and utensils. So grab Grandma's well seasoned cast iron skillet, a cut of meat with marbling that would make a Greek blush.
Add some kosher salt and a couple cloves of garlic and you're ready to get cooking!
